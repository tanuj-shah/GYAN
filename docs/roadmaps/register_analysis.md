### 1. Friction Reduction: Real-Time Password Strength
*   **Problem**: Users only find out their password is "weak" or doesn't match after a full page reload or form submission.
*   **Solution**: Implement a visual password strength meter (Red to Green) and a real-time "Match" indicator for the confirmation field.
*   **Impact**: Simplifies the technical hurdles of joining and prevents frustrating submission errors.

### 2. Trust Amplification: Member Testimonial Snippet
*   **Enhancement**: Place a small, high-impact testimonial from a current "Country Lead" or prominent member near the form.
*   **Impact**: Seeing a real face and name attached to the "Why Join" story makes the benefits feel tangible rather than theoretical.

### 3. Professional Validation: Work Email Incentive
*   **Lead-In**: Encourage users to "Register with your professional/work email for faster verification."
*   **Logic**: Signals that GYAN is an elite professional network, not just a casual social site.

### 4. Immediate Engagement: The "Day 0" Preview
*   **Onboarding**: Add a small "What happens next?" section (e.g., 1. Verify Email, 2. Complete Profile, 3. Connect with your Chapter).
*   **Impact**: Removes the "black hole" feeling after registration and sets clear expectations for the new member.
